REGISTRATIONS

After your paper is accepted, you can begin the registration process. At least one author must register for the paper to be included in the proceedings and the Technical Program. Please strictly follow the registration deadline mentioned your acceptance mail.

  • Indian or SAARC Nations authors / participants can pay the registration amount in INR using Razorpay link given below.
  • Non-Indian Authors / participants can pay through PayPal as given below.
  • All the below Registration Fees are inclusive of Taxes and Bank Transfer/Platform charges (If any). In the payment link below all charges are included in the registration amount

REGISTRATION INCLUDES:

  • The registration fee for the regular papers includes the Article Processing Charge (APC) to be paid to the Publisher for all the accepted and presented papers to publish the proceedings after the conference. Hence there will be no additional fee to be paid by the authors after the conference.
  • Welcome reception, Conference Badge
  • Conference Kit.
  • Presentation certificate.
  • Access to all technical sessions and Keynotes.
  • Snacks, Tea/Coffee and Lunch.
  • The conference is non-residential and delegates are kindly requested to make their own arrangement for accommodation.

IMPORTANT POINTS :

  • To be eligible for student registration, the presenting author should be a student.
  • The above registration fees are inclusive of taxes.
  • Once the paper is registered, withdrawal is not allowed under any circumstances.
