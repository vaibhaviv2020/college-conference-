SPECIAL SESSIONS

About Special Sessions

Special sessions at Conference 2026 provide a focused forum for researchers and practitioners to present and discuss cutting-edge topics in Artificial Intelligence, Computation, Communication, and Network Security. These sessions enable deeper exploration of emerging areas and facilitate networking among experts in specialized domains.

Researchers are invited to propose special session themes aligned with the conference scope. Each special session should comprise a minimum of 5-6 papers. Proposals must include a session title, rationale, list of topics, and organizer(s) information.

Papers accepted for presentation in special sessions will be published in the conference proceedings. Honorariums will be provided for registered papers presented at special sessions, subject to conference policies.

Contact for Special Session Proposals:
Email: sessions@conference2026.edu
Phone: +91-20-XXXX-XXXX

Important Notes

  • Each special session proposal must include a minimum of 5 papers.
  • Submission deadline for special session proposals: 15 January 2026.
  • Session organizers must be registered for the conference.
  • Papers will undergo the same peer-review process as regular submissions.
