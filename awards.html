AWARDS

Awards & Recognition

To honor outstanding research, motivate scholarly excellence, and recognize outstanding presentations, the conference committee will present the following awards during the closing ceremony.

Best Paper Award

Awarded to the most outstanding research paper submitted to the regular tracks. Evaluation is based on reviewers' scores, technical depth, originality, and impact.

Best Student Paper

Presented to the best paper where the primary author and presenter is a full-time student. Designed to encourage and support young researchers entering the field.

Best Presentation

Awarded to the presenter with the most engaging, clear, and structured presentation in each technical track session, as evaluated by session chairs.
